I developed a website system for the 19th Indonesian Obstetrics and Gynecology Congress (KOGI), which will be held on July 17–23, 2025, at the Bali Nusa Dua Convention Center (BNDCC). This project is part of my duties at [Inovasi Al-Fatih Maulana](https://inovasialfatih.com), Ciamis. Its main features include a symposium and workshop ticket purchasing system, abstract submission, and schedule and speaker information. The website was built using the Laravel framework.
