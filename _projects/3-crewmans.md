The ship crew management system for PT. Samudera Indonesia was developed in collaboration with [Ahmad Fauzi Lubis](https://github.com/Ahmadfzlbs) and [Anggi Prayoga](https://github.com/anggiprayoga9). Its features include crew & vessel management, promotion & demotion, finances, and medical check-ups. It was created using Next.js.
